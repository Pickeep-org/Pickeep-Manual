Pickeep - User manual



Welcome

Welcome to PicKeep! Our app is meant for generous and eco-friendly people who want to share their unused stuff with each other for free. In this manual we'll discover the secrets of our new exclusive app.

Signing up

First, when you open the app you see our welcome screen. If it's your first time, you can sign up or connect with google or with your phone number. For signing up with email, Make sure you write your email correctly and choose a strong password. Please notice that you need to verify your email by following the mail you get from us.







Sign in with phone

For signing in with phone, make sure you have an Israeli number and fill it without the country code (for example - 05XXXXXXXX).







Sign in with Google:

For signing in with your Google account, first log in to that account on your device.







Sign in with email

If you already signed up with your email, just put your email and password.







Contact information

When signing in with one of the methods above for the first time, you'll need to fill up your information so you can easily publish your items and even pick and keep an item that you liked. If some of your information change in the future, don't worry, you can always edit your profile.







Forgot password

You already have signed up and forgot your password? Just press the button and you'll get an email for resetting it right away.







Home screen

Congratulations, now you're logged in and can see all the published items that waiting for you to pick and keep them. You can filter the items by categories, whole districts or by specific cities. You can combine both filters for better and specific results.







Viewing an item, picking and keeping it

When you view other user's items, you can contact the owner by phone, SMS or WhatsApp and navigate to pick and keep it. You can also look up more items published by this generous user.







Sharing an item

When you view other user's items, if you like it and want to share with your friends, just push the button right above.







Favorite list

Liked an item but you can't contact the owner right now? Add it to your favorites and view this list any time on the app's home screen just by clicking on the right tab.







Publishing an item

If you choose to give an item that you don't need anymore and add it to the app, all you need to do is press the plus button on home screen and write its name and description. If the item is not at your current location, You can change the address as well. Finally, in order to help others find your item, choose up to three categories that describe the item the best and add a photo either from your gallery or by taking a picture with your camera.







Editing an item

Whenever you want to edit an item you've already published, just go to your item through home screen (You can use your own items tab for easily locate your items) and perform the action you want.







Deleting an item

If you decide to keep an item you've already published and not let others picking it, you can delete this item completely, but you better delete it after someone picked and kept it.







Editing your contact information

Moved an apartment? Changed your phone number? Decided to make a fresh start with a brand new name? Just click your profile on the home screen and change what you need.







Accessiblity for the visually Impaired people

We believe that anyone should have the chance to pick and keep the items published in our app, so we made adjustments comepetible with the accessibility app so everything is readable. For example, on home screen the filters active right now are being read automatically and taping on an item will read the item's name.







Thank you!
